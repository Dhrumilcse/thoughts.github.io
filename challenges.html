Do challenges work?

You will be seeing this as my first post, but the fact is it isn't. I write for some time, then I take a pause and seldom come back until after 3-4-5 months. That is a bad habit.


To break this habit, I decided to challenge myself on writing 250-300 meaningful words every 3 days (enough time to research/think & write), focusing more on things I like, random good stuff to make your day better if you are reading, and sometimes the bad to clear some headspace. There is no clear idea at this moment, but I am least concerned about it, as I will be writing pretending no one is reading.


So, do challenges work? I guess we will find out.
