Poodcasts

I loooove podcasts. Recently I came across a podcast accidentally and since then I regularly (trust me, I try) listen to them. You not only learn new things but more importantly you learn how to be an active listener. Podcasts are free (Podcasts on iOS, Google Podcasts on Android,) you just download the episode and listen on the go.

1

THE SCHOOL OF GREATNESS

LEWIS HOWES


It is a great source of positivity. More than 600 episodes till now and each with a new guest. The one with Jay Shetty is my favourite. I learned how to aproach almost anything and everything with optimism.

2

WorkLife

ADAM GRANT


Adam Grant is an American Psycologist. I personally admire him and Sheryl— COO of Facebook — after reading their book Originals: How Non-Conformists Move the World. The work they are doing in and out of their respective fields is absolutely amazing.

3

Curiosity Daily

CURIOSITY.COM


Your daily dose of interesting things, research which is shorter than your commute. Listen and Learn — in just a few minutes!

4

moneycontrol Podcast

moneycontrol.com


If, like me, you are interested in financial markets but don't have enough time to watch market the entire day here is your thing.
